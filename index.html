Our Tools

Powerful. Intelligent. Revolutionary.

Each tool in the Cirkitly stack is designed to work seamlessly together or independently, giving you the flexibility to build your perfect hardware workflow.

● Available Now

spec2rtl

Transform natural language specifications into verified, synthesizable RTL with autonomous validation and testing. Your AI copilot for hardware design.

● Coming Soon

picoplace

CLI-driven PCB layout synthesis with AI-powered placement optimization and real-time visualization. From netlist to layout in seconds.

rtl-copilot

cuda-genie

AI compiler that transforms C/Python algorithms into optimized CUDA kernels and HLS designs with automatic performance tuning.

silicon-agent

Complete workflow orchestration from specification to layout. The ultimate end-to-end automation for silicon development.
